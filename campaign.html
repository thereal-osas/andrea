CREATE YOUR OWN FUNDRAISING CAMPAIGN

ABF launches its crowdfunding platform!

We always believe that together we can do a lot, but united we can do much more, the Foundation has created a fundraising platform in which you yourself can be the protagonist and promoter!

How?

1. CHOOSE A PROJECT TO SUPPORT,2. CREATE YOUR OWN FUNDRAISING CAMPAIGN,3. PROMOTE IT THROUGH YOUR NETWORK OF CONTACTS.

When?

ALWAYS

Whether it is on the occasion of an anniversary (birthday), or a special day (graduation), or simply because you want to be an active part of the big ABF family, it is always the right time to activate your fundraising campaign!

How much?

EVERY DROP IS PRECIOUS!

It only takes a few euros to donate a study book, or a few more to donate a tablet. You can choose a project and a collection goal so your contacts can help you with more motivation to achieve it.

Who?

REALLY EVERYONE!

From age 18 to 99+, there are no limits to your willingness to do good. You can decide to activate the campaign individually or even as a group, family, or company.

Where?

ANYWHERE!

You can promote the fundraiser by sharing it on social media, but also on whatsapp or by email or by a simple word of mouth! Every means of communication can be used to share your initiative.

So, what are you waiting for?

Start here!

Why? WITH YOU, NOW MORE THAN EVER, TO GIVE OPPORTUNITY AND HOPE TO SO MANY CHILDREN!
