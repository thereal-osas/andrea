ANDREA BOCELLI FOUNDATION

Mission

The Andrea Bocelli Foundation was created to empower people and communities in situations of poverty, illiteracy and distress due to illness and social exclusion. We do this by promoting and supporting projects in Italy and abroad that help overcome these barriers and drive people to express their full potential.

Vision

“Our faith in love and justice calls upon us to build a better world than the one we have found. It calls upon us to give back to the world all the good things we have received, so the less fortunate or most vulnerable members of our society get a chance to achieve a life full of opportunity and beauty. So those who take up the challenge will find concrete opportunities and the energy required to thrive in their own lives.” Andrea Bocelli

Our story

The Foundation was established in July 2011 inspired by the desire of Maestro Bocelli and his family to give back a measure of the love and support that they had received over the course of his career spent traveling around the world.

Believing that “unity signifies strength and that we can do much alone, but much more together,” it was natural to transform this concept into a structured approach by establishing a Foundation that could provide tangible aid to others.

Today,the Andrea Bocelli Foundation strives to be a living laboratory. One that is made up of many individual, yet generous members who come together from every corner of the world to work drive our mission and impact forward.

Combining forces is the successful mission that lies behind this exciting project: to tangibly help our neighbors, by achieving the priceless privilege of making ourselves useful by providing the best possible quality support.

Our Values

The Andrea Bocelli Foundation works in strict adherence to a selection of key values, which can be summed up in the following five “Cs”:

Courage to believe in change
Care or caring for people that we support, becoming aware of their needs and unexpressed strengths
Concreteness in providing tangible and measurable solutions
Competence to ensure our actions are efficient
Cooperation to bring together aspiration and requirements, through philanthropic aid activities so that anyone, young or old, can participate and make their own small contribution leveraging human, material and/or economic resources.

The key value that sums up our 5Cs is TRUST. The trust we aspire to and the trust we ask for, the trust we give, and the trust we live by.
